Documentation

Learn how to use CIFAKE for AI-generated image detection

Getting Started

1. Create an Account - Sign up with your email to access the analysis dashboard

2. Upload an Image - Select or drag-and-drop a JPG, PNG, or WEBP image (max 10MB)

3. Review Results - View prediction confidence, Grad-CAM heatmap, and detailed analysis

4. Export Report - Download a comprehensive forensic analysis report

Supported Image Formats

JPEG/JPG - Recommended for photos (max 10MB)

PNG - Best for high-quality images with transparency

WebP - Modern format with optimal compression

All images are processed at 32×32 resolution for analysis

Understanding Results

Confidence Score

The model outputs a confidence percentage (0-100%) for AI generation likelihood

Grad-CAM Heatmap

Red/warm areas indicate pixels that influenced the AI detection decision. Brighter areas are more significant.

Processing Time

Typical inference takes 0.8 seconds from upload to results

Important Limitations

  • • CIFAKE was trained on images from specific AI generators. Results may vary on other sources.
  • • Highly compressed images or extreme post-processing may affect accuracy
  • • The model achieves 92.98% accuracy - some false positives/negatives are expected
  • • Results should be verified by human experts in critical applications
  • • Not suitable for forensic evidence in court without additional verification

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image stored?

No. Images are processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We do not retain any image data.

How accurate is the model?

The model achieves 92.98% accuracy on the CIFAKE test set, but real-world accuracy may vary.

Can I export results?

Yes. All predictions can be exported as PDF reports with full analysis details.

What about bulk analysis?

Contact our sales team for enterprise API access and batch processing capabilities.
